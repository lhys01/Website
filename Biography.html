Biography

Roberto was born in 1973 on October 31st in Palermo, Italy. Without very much money and no sports he didn't have a entertaining childhood. During his teen years he got a Vespa scotter which gave him a little more freedom. He went to the University of Palermo and majored in Statistics. He then moved to Dublin, Ireland and lived there as a stock broker. After living there for seven years he moved to London. This is where he met his future wife. Once he met Megan Schuster he wasn't sure about getting serious. He choose to get engaged and gave her an engagement braclet instead of a ring. Then they made the choice to start a family. On June 30th 2010 his life changed with the birth of his son. After living in London and Italy with his son Rhys then the Ferrito's moved to Pasadena. Once they moved Roberto started running a lot and frequently. He would have a race every weekend and train with about five-ten miles a day. He took two days off in a year becuase he was flying and couldn't run. He did fifty half marathons and 250 races in a seven year period. He best time for a half marathon was one hour and twenty two mintues which was considered eltie for his age group. After his big running days were over he foucused on Rhys's running carreer. In 2021 his life changed again when he adopted a dog named Luna .

Rhys then quickly shifted to running insprired by his dad. he started running about ten miles a week at 8 years old. His first race was Janurary 2018 and it was a one mile race. His first race happened to be his first win. After that he began to run more and run faster. He did his first 5k also in January 2018. After that they moved to Reno, Nevada where Rhys had his 9th birthday. He starts running more and more and goes to the Junior Olympics for cross country. Rhys then switchs to triatholon where he can train his endurance. When it is time for Rhys' first triatholon nationals he gets 7th place. Now he did more triatholons and consistanlly winning kids ones. He also won a few adult ones too. In time for his second tri nationals he got third in the U.S. After training even more everyday he got first place at nationals for non draft legal.
